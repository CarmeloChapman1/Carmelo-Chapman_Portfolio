Professional Vision

How I see my future in technology and the kind of professional I want to become.

Looking Ahead

My long-term vision is to become a well-rounded technologist who can move confidently between software engineering, data, and security. I want to be the kind of person who can understand the technical details, but also explain them clearly to people who don’t work in IT every day.

Working inside a healthcare organization has shown me that technology is not just about writing code — it is about reliability, trust, and impact. When systems work, people get the care and information they need. My goal is to contribute to systems that matter, where the work I do helps real people in meaningful ways.

As I continue my career, I plan to keep learning new tools and approaches, but I also want to stay grounded in good communication, patience, and problem-solving. My vision is to grow into roles where I can both build solutions and help others understand and use them.
