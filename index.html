Introduction

I am part of the 27-month BlueSky Tennessee Institute program, where I complete a Bachelor of Science in Computing from ETSU while working year-round inside BlueCross BlueShield of Tennessee. Through rotations in Cloud Operations & Containers, Data Management, and Membership & Enrollment, I’ve been able to see how technology supports healthcare at scale.

I’m especially interested in how software engineering, data, and security intersect. Outside of class and work, I enjoy building baseball-themed projects, learning more about cybersecurity tools, and experimenting with game development in Unity.
