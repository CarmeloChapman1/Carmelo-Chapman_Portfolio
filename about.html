About Me

A Computing student blending real-world industry experience with academic projects in software, data, and security.

Background

I grew up interested in technology and how systems work behind the scenes. The BlueSky Tennessee Institute has given me a unique opportunity to study Computing at an accelerated pace while working directly at BlueCross BlueShield of Tennessee.

On the academic side, I am completing a Bachelor of Science in Computing, with coursework in programming, databases, networking, and cybersecurity. On the professional side, I’ve rotated through different IT teams, seeing how code, data, and infrastructure come together inside a large healthcare organization.

I value clear communication, patience, and adaptability. Whether I’m sending emails to vendors, collaborating with teammates, or learning a new tool, I try to stay curious and focused on the bigger picture of how my work supports the team.
