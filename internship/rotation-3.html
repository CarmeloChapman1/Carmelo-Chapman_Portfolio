Rotation III: Membership & Enrollment

Supporting open enrollment activities through vendor communication, file coordination, and job submissions.

Team & Focus

In this rotation, I worked with the commercial enrollment processing team. The group supports open enrollment (OE) activities, vendor coordination, and the processing of member enrollment data so coverage remains accurate and timely.

Key Responsibilities

  • Sent emails to vendors to coordinate open enrollment files and timelines.
  • Helped track which groups needed OE files and which vendors were responsible.
  • Submitted jobs in ServiceNow related to enrollment processing.

Tools & Technologies

  • Outlook for vendor and internal communication.
  • Excel and vendor listing documents for tracking contacts and groups.
  • ServiceNow for submitting and tracking related jobs.
  • Internal membership and enrollment systems.

Problem-Solving Example

One situation that stands out was when a vendor did not respond to emails for several days. Instead of waiting indefinitely, I took the initiative to identify the correct contact and escalated the communication. This helped get the process moving again and reinforced how important it is to be proactive when timelines are involved.

Reflection

This rotation pushed me to grow in my communication skills. I became more comfortable emailing people I had never met before, summarizing what was needed, and following up when something stalled. I also saw how even small tasks, like sending a clear email or submitting a job correctly, contribute to keeping enrollment data accurate for members.
